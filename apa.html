Alpha Phi Alpha - Eta Nu Chapter

Alpha Phi Alpha, the first intercollegiate Greek-letter fraternity established for African American Men, was founded at Cornell University in Ithaca, New York by seven college men who recognized the need for a strong bond of brotherhood among African descendants in this country. Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity Inc. was founded on December 4, 1906 at Cornell University in Ithaca, NY. The Eta Nu Chapter was chartered here on the campus of East Carolina University on April 3, 1971
